OUR PHILOSOPHY

Meaning life, water, island, bird in Greek, AVA MediterrAegean is a celebration of splendor, freedom, escapism and joy, where genuine hospitality, delectable food and vibrant atmosphere combine to entertain and illuminate the senses and transport guests to celebrate the night away. The multi sensory venue features the true essences of Mediterranean and Aegean cultures, design and cuisines combined to offer the most transporting dining experience in a refined and spirited atmosphere. The distinctively curated space pays homage to the simple spirit of Cycladic design. Embracing the effortless beauty of the ﬁve elements, AVA MediterrAegean offers a unique experience that immerses all the senses, through reﬁned taste, bespoke mixology, transporting ethnic sounds and unparalleled ambiance.
